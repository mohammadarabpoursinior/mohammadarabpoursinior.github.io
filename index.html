Our Technology

Targeted Mucosal Platform

Our technology combines a modular antigen design with targeted immune activation Nextgen Vax proprietory solution. It is built to enhance local and systemic immunity while reducing the burden associated with traditional needle-based administration.

The platform integrates immune-enhancing components, disease-relevant targets, and guidance elements within a nanoparticle-based framework to improve uptake, response, and development flexibility across multiple indications.
